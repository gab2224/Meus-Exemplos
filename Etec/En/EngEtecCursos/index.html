High School with Professional Qualification in Systems Development Technician

 

He is the professional who analyzes and designs systems. Builds, documents, tests and maintains information systems. It uses development environments and specific programming languages. Models, implements and maintains databases.  

 

Where to work

High School with Training Itinerary - Languages, Humanities and Social Sciences

 

High School classes with an emphasis on Languages, Humanities and Social Sciences are held part-time (morning or afternoon). By choosing this modality, the student has the opportunity to delve into oral and written language, exercise intellectual and expressive skills, learn the mother tongue and foreign languages, as well as study man from a social, cultural and historical point of view. Upon completion of high school with an emphasis on Languages, Humanities and Social Sciences, the student will be able to pursue higher education studies preferably in the areas of: Administration, Architecture and Humanism, Arts, Audiovisual, Information and Communication Sciences, Humanities and Social Sciences, among others.

Administration Technician

How organizations work, from private companies to NGOs, through public agencies, commerce and industry. The student will need knowledge of Portuguese, mathematics, history and geography to understand the main subjects of the course, such as the history of administration, evolution of organizations over time, accounting, laws that regulate the operation of companies and writing documents. The student will also learn to analyze the chances of a business or product being successful and consumer behavior. You will also study customer service techniques, entrepreneurship (initiatives to do new business) and how an organization plans to achieve its goals and sets its goals for the future.

Building Technician

 

Description
The main subjects of the course are Design, for the elaboration and development of engineering and architectural projects, Construction Materials Technology, to select and control the quality of materials, Construction Technology, to supervise and improve the construction processes, Planning and Control of the Works, to ensure compliance with deadlines and costs. Among other subjects, the student uses Mathematics, to calculate proportions, areas and volumes, and Chemistry, for the student to understand the properties of materials.

 

  Performance

 

In offices, it assists and prepares architectural and civil engineering projects, within the regulated limits. You can also work with technical advice or technology consultant.

Nursing Technician

 

Description
The student learns techniques to assist in the recovery of a patient, as well as disease prevention, guidance for exams, among other procedures. The course is based on biology, to learn about the human body; in mathematics, for measurement calculations for medications; in physics, to understand the risks that the patient runs, such as the speed of a vehicle crash to understand the types of possible injuries, for example; and Portuguese, to write correctly in the patient's chart.
performance
The technician performs an assistance role for the nurse, such as basic care in critically ill patients, vaccine application, bathing a patient, assisting in exams, among other functions. He can work in the administrative area, in prevention and can set up educational actions, in the area of ​​occupational safety.

Marketing Technician

The student will learn that marketing is more than advertising. It is a process that begins with understanding consumer needs to develop products, services and ideas that will be marketed. To study this subject, the student will need knowledge of Portuguese, geography and mathematics. The student will learn to define the target audience of each product or service, as well as learn to analyze this process of approximation and exchange between sellers and buyers. The student will also study ways to use communication tools, such as advertising, telemarketing, sales promotion, etc. In the course, the student will also learn to create logos, labels and packaging. RH Technician

Description The student will need knowledge of Portuguese, mathematics and history to understand the course and learn to perform tasks under the responsibility of the Human Resources department of a company, such as hiring, firing and retiring employees and offering benefits -transportation, health insurance, meal vouchers, etc.), among other activities. Therefore, it will also be important to study the laws that regulate the rights and duties of employers and employees (labor legislation). The student will also learn notions of psychology to understand how people relate to each other in the work environment, carry out recruitment and selection processes for new employees and promote motivational actions.

Chemistry Technician

Description
Mathematics is used for the basis of calculations. Physical chemistry is also an important subject in the course. The student will learn to manipulate instruments such as a microscope, analytical balance and other equipment aimed at chemical analysis. Another important point is the understanding of industrial process technology, where students learn the chemical transformation and evolution of the raw material to the final product.
Practice
Can work in several areas as a process analyst or supervisor. He analyzes the quality and purity of the raw material that arrives at the company. He can also develop products and work with quality control in chemical processes..

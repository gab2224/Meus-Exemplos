Message from Our School!


Who said you can't learn about friendship at school? It is at school that we spend most of our childhood time, and there we make the great discoveries about life. It is at school that we learn new things, that we fall in love for the first time, and that we make friends that will mark our lives forever, even though they may not remain in our lives. School friends are companions on the journey, learning, adventures and mischief. Then, when we grow up and leave school, we always have childhood stories to tell, and there those friends are part of our lives again. But there are some classmates who end up becoming our best friends. In the memory of childhood friends, we will always find good memories and beautiful stories about ourselves. But it's really good, it's when the best friends from school can stay in our lives, and keep writing stories together with us. Back to school is always a time of great hope and joy. Joy to welcome our students back, to have a full house again. Hope for the new school year to come. We hope that everyone has a good return and that this journey begins with great happiness. This is the time to see friends, teachers and focus on having a year of great learning and knowledge. Today is the beginning of a new time that we are sure will be unique and amazing. Welcome, students, parents and teachers and have a good return
